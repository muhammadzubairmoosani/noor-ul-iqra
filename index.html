Why Choose Us?

Online Quran academy has been enlightening Muslims about the teachings of the holy Quran and making their lives according to the principles of Islam. We take pride in what we do and we take it upon ourselves to make our brothers and sisters good Muslims. With a step by step process designed by keeping in mind the objective of teaching our students correctly, we aim to make you learn Quran with tajweed comprehensively.

  • 3 Days Free Trial Classes

  • Special focus on slow learners
  • Online Quran memorization
  • Female Quran teachers for women and children
  • Expert Quran teachers with English fluency
  • Monthly progress assessment and feedback
  • Timings that suit you
  • Different Islamic and Quranic courses if you want
