Why Choose Noor Ul Iqra Academy?

At Noor Ul Iqra Academy, our Online Quran Academy has been dedicated to enlightening Muslims on the teachings of the Holy Quran. We focus on imparting Quranic knowledge and Islamic principles to our students in a methodical manner. Our goal is to help you learn the Quran with Tajweed in a comprehensive and accessible way.

  • 3 Days Free Trial Classes for Quran Learning
  • Word-by-word Quran Learning with Tajweed
  • Special Focus on Slow Learners for Better Understanding
  • Online Quran Memorization Courses
  • Female Quran Teachers Available for Women and Children
  • Expert Quran Teachers Fluent in English
  • Monthly Progress Assessment and Personalized Feedback
  • Flexible Timings that Suit Your Schedule
  • Wide Range of Islamic and Quranic Courses Available

Popular Quran and Islamic Courses

Explore Our Wide Range of Quran and Islamic Courses

Basic Qaida Course

The Basic Qaida Course is perfect for beginners, whether kids or adults, who wish to start learning the Quran from scratch.

Quran Reading Course

Our Quran Reading Course helps students improve their Quran reading skills with correct Tajweed.

Quran Memorization Course

This Quran Memorization Course is designed for students who wish to memorize the entire Quran by heart (Hifz).

Quran Translation Course

The Quran Translation Course helps students understand the Quran verse-by-verse in Urdu for better comprehension.

Rules of Tajweed Course

Our Rules of Tajweed Course is designed to help students, both male and female, learn the correct rules of Tajweed.

Islamic Studies for Kids

The Islamic Studies for Kids Course is specially designed for Muslim children to learn the basics of Islam in an engaging way.
